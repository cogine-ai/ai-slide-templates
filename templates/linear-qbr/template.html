Quarter review brief

This QBR summarizes Mercury Consulting's Q2 operating performance and the decisions needed for Q3.

  2. Pipeline quality improved while enterprise buying cycles remained slower than expected.
  3. Delivery capacity is the main constraint for onboarding new strategic accounts.
  4. Q3 priorities focus on margin discipline, customer health, and partner-led growth.
