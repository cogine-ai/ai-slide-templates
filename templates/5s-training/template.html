Introduction

This session prepares shop-floor teams to reset a shared packing cell, reduce search time, and keep the workspace audit-ready after every shift.

What is 5S?

A disorganized workplace is, at best, an inefficient one. At worst, it can be an unsafe environment for everyone involved.

Staying organized helps keep things running safely and smoothly.

Enter 5S - an organization system developed in Japan that focuses on creating a clean, efficient, and safe work environment.

5S Checklist

Sort

Remove obsolete labels, broken tools, and unused packaging from the bench.

Set in order

Label tool positions so the next operator can find everything within 30 seconds.

Shine

Clean the surface, inspect cables, and record any maintenance concerns.

Standardize

Use the same photo standard and end-of-shift checklist across all lanes.

Sustain

Review audit scores weekly and assign one owner for unresolved actions.

Workplace Benefits

Improved safety, faster handoffs, fewer missing tools, cleaner work areas, and a stronger culture of ownership.
