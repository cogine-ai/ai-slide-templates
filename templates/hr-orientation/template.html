WELCOME
MESSAGE

Welcome to Brightpath Studio. This orientation gives every new teammate the same starting map: who we serve, how we work, and where to get help.

By the end of today, you will know your first-week priorities, key contacts, required tools, and the norms that keep our work clear and respectful.

NEW HIRE
ROADMAP

Your first month is organized around learning the product, meeting your working group, completing compliance setup, and shipping one small supported contribution.

KEY POLICIES

BENEFITS

Medical, dental, and vision plans begin on your first day of employment.

Support

MEET THE
TEAM

Introduce managers, buddies, and support contacts with a visual-first page.

SUPPORT MAP

People Ops owns payroll, benefits, and policy questions.

For urgent support, message #people-help before 3 PM local time. For sensitive concerns, email people@brightpath.example and request a private follow-up.

CHECKLIST

Finish forms, enroll benefits, and confirm direct deposit.

Complete the onboarding checklist by Friday so payroll, equipment, compliance, and learning access are all active before your first project sprint.

Q AND A

Bring questions about working norms, tools, or team rituals.

The facilitator will collect open questions, route owner-specific answers, and post a written recap in the orientation channel after the session.

NEXT STEPS

Book manager, buddy, and cross-functional intro meetings.

Use the calendar links in your onboarding email to schedule three first-week conversations and confirm your 30-day learning objective.

TOOLS SETUP

Activate SSO, Slack, project tracker, and knowledge base access.

After each login works, add recovery methods, join required channels, and pin the onboarding workspace so daily resources are easy to find.

CULTURE NOTES

Default to clear ownership, written context, and respectful feedback.

Decisions should name the owner, deadline, and next checkpoint. When context is missing, ask directly and make the answer visible to the team.

HELP DESK

Use one intake path for equipment, account, and access issues.

Submit requests through help.brightpath.example so Facilities, IT, and People Operations can triage quickly and keep status updates in one place.
